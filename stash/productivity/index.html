Productivity

created: Wed, 19 Aug 2020; modified: Wed, 19 Aug 2020 ; reading time: 1 mins

I’ve included Productivity as an aspect. It encompasses all the things that “Make it easyier for later”. The next time you have to do that thing it’ll be easier as you’ve make an effort now to setup a few things to reduce the effort and overhead of having to do it all again.
