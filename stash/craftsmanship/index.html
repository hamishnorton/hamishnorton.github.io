Craftsmanship

created: Wed, 19 Aug 2020; modified: Tue, 25 Aug 2020 ; reading time: 1 mins

I didn’t really give my craft the focus it deserved until I discovered my purpose. One’s purpose tends to evolve with time.

purpose: Make It Easier For Others
craft: Software Solution Development

Once I’d started really applying myself to my craft I noticed that a lot of what I had been percieving situation problems fell away. Decissions at work became easier to make, as I could simply ask myself as a craftsman what is the right thing to do. You’ll notice I added the word Solution into my craft as I’m not here to just write code, I’m here to develop solutions that make a difference to the people that use them and provide value.
